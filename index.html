About me

I’m a confident hat wearer (despite a statistically small head). Not just physically — professionally too.

I move comfortably between strategy, system design and hands-on engineering, helping organisations build digital platforms that are both elegant and sustainable.

With over a decade in the industry, my focus has shifted from pages to systems — from visuals to governance.

Good digital work isn’t just how something looks on launch day. It’s how well it functions, adapts and survives in year three.
